Internal Transfer

Requirements

If the employee applicant meets the minimum competencies for the position, the employee applicant will be scheduled to interview with the hiring manager.
In addition to meeting the minimum competencies for the position, employee applicant must meet the following criteria:
1. The employee applicant must be in the current position for twelve(12) months;
2. The employee applicant must be rated as S(Satisfactory) or above in the latest performance rating cycle;
3. An employee applicant on a Performance Improvement Plan(PIP) may not apply for a new position or transfer.

Software Engineer

IT Division

Responsible for designing, developing, testing and maintaining software applications.

Job Description

  • Develop efficient and reliable code.
  • Participate in requirements analysis, system design and architecture planning.
  • Perform unit testing and integration testing to ensure software quality.
  • Collaborate with cross-departmental teams to solve technical problems.

Required skills

Proficiency in at least one programming language (e.g., Python, Java, JavaScript), familiarity with version control tools (e.g., Git), and good problem-solving skills are required.

Project Manager

APAC Division

Responsible for planning, executing and monitoring projects to ensure they are completed on schedule and within budget.

Job Description

  • Define project scope, objectives, and deliverables.
  • Manage project timelines, resources, and risks.
  • Coordinate internal teams with external stakeholders.
  • Report project progress to management regularly.

Required skills

Excellent communication and organizational skills, familiarity with project management methodologies (such as Agile, Scrum), and leadership skills.

Marketing Specialist

Marketing Division

Responsible for planning and executing marketing activities to enhance brand awareness and product sales.

Job Description

  • Develop marketing strategies and plans.
  • Manage social media platforms and content distribution.
  • Analyze market trends and competitors.
  • Execute online or offline promotional activities.

Required skills

Possess market analysis skills, familiarity with digital marketing tools (e.g. Google Analytics, SEO), and possess creative and copywriting skills.
